Dream Car Simulator

React.JS App

This is my first React.JS/Node.JS project - It's a simple car simulation app where the user could input the information for their car to build their dream car. The user then would be able to simulate their driving experience by trying out different functions within the car. The simulator page has a panel of functional elements including:

  • A light switch to turn on and off the car headlights
  • Gas pedal to accelerate
  • Brake to slow down the speed

Epic Battleship

Javascript/jQuery App

This is a pair-programmed Javascript app where the user plays against the computer, which has placed the ships randomly across the board. The ships are in varying lengths depending the type of the ship taking up from 1 to 5 blocks on the board. You can track the number of "Hits" and "Misses" from the panel that indicates the number of torpedoes left and ships destroyed. There's also a "Ship Life Status" table that tracks the number of "Hits" and indicates the type of ship that has been hit.
